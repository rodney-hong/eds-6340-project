## Midterm Project Presentation Guidelines

Midterm Project presentation guidelines:

- **Time limit:** 15 minutes for the entire group.
- **Order of presentation:** Introduction and Data Scraping, EDA, Data Visualization, Data Mining, Conclusion, and Implementation.
- **Delivery:** Speak clearly and explain the part of the project that you completed. Don't read from a text.
- **Be prepared:** Practice explaining your work, timing it, and the strategies you used to complete your part.
- **Setting:** All members need to gather in one location, using one laptop equipped with a webcam and mic, with MS Teams Meeting access.
- **Video and Audio:** I need to see and hear you clearly during your presentation.
- **Methodology and Approach:** Focus on the methodology you followed for your section, not the specific code you used.
- **Conclusion and Analysis:** Since your time is limited, you do not need to show every piece of information you collected. Focus on the key insights and takeaways.
- **Q & A:** As a team member, you need to know about the entire project. Therefore, be prepared to answer any question asked about the entire project.
